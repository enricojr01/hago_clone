List of valid appointment statuses:

NoShow - default, so that if a patient doesn't show up we don't have to do anything special to change their status.
Registered - showed up at clinic, paid, and is waiting
Completed - consultation / procedure complete
CancelledByPatient - Cancelled by patient
CancelledByStaff - Cancelled by staff

Better ones:
AWAITING - Waiting for confirmation
CONFIRMED - Confirmed
COMPLETED - Patient visited and the appointment went well 
NOSHOW - Patient missed the appointment
CANCELLED_USER - User cancelled the appointment
CANCELLED_CLINIC - Clinic cancelled the appointment
